ONGC at a Glance

Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC), a Fortune Global 500 company, is India’s premier organization engaged in exploration and exploitation of Hydrocarbons. With its registered office at Deendayal Urja Bhavan, Vasant Kunj in New Delhi, the E&P activities are spread through the length and breadth of the country. It is a fully integrated Oil & Gas company in India, operating along the entire hydrocarbon value chain.

ONGC currently produces around 24 MMTPA of crude oil and 25 BCM of natural gas along with around 3600 KT of value-added products from 17 of its Assets in India and through its JV partners. Being in the upstream business of producing oil and gas, ONGC undertakes business activities in the exploration, development, drilling, production, and reservoir management to discover and produce hydrocarbons from its existing assets and basins. There is lot of data that gets generated while executing the business activities from 17 Assets.
